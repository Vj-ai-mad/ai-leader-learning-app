## Summary

- **46 total modules** across 5 stages — comfortably within the 42-56 target
- **~39 Original / ~7 Link** — heavily weighted toward original content, concentrated where you have the most differentiated expertise (Stage 4)
- At 15-20 min/day, this covers roughly **9-10 weeks** if done 5 days/week — slightly longer than the original 6-8 week estimate, which is fine for a tester phase (better to under-promise on pace)

## Next Step: Generating the Actual Content

For each "Original" module, feed the title + one-line intent (from this table) into Bedrock with a prompt like:

> "Write an original 300-500 word lesson for a Program Manager/Delivery Manager/Platform Lead/RTE on the topic '[title]'. Assume no technical background beyond general software delivery experience. End with one practical takeaway they can apply this week. Do not reference or summarize any specific external article — this should be original instructional content."

For each "Link" module, find one genuinely good free resource, then generate a short 100-150 word original framing intro connecting it to a delivery leader's context.
